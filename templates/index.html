As my beloved hound dog Elvis would tell you, David Deal is — first and foremost — a proud father to both two-legged and four-legged children. But what else?

My personal pursuits do have some overlap with this. I am always trying to broaden my core foundations with Python and other languages. Lately, that has led me to experiment with Flask, the framework with which this site is being built. And like everyone else these days I have been captivated by the possibilities of AI.
